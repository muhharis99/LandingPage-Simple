WELCOME TO THE
ISLAND OF COCONUTS.


Indonesia is the World's largest producer of coconuts, with its gross production of 20 million tonnes in 2020 according to Food and Agriculture Organization of the United Nations: Statistical Division (FAOSTAT). With the abundance of coconut in the nation, you have come to the right place choosing Indonesia as your source-destination for a consistent supply of your all your Coconut Products needs.


Almost every part of Coconut can be use in our life. It's like a "Swiss Army Knife" of the plant kingdom; in one neat package it provides a high-calorie food from its meat, delicious and refreshing water, fiber that can be spun into rope, a hard shell that can be turned into charcoal and it can produce a healthy oil that has a lot of benefits for our life. What's more, until it is needed for some other purpose, it serves as a handy floatation device.


LANDING has a mission to create awareness and share the many benefits of processed coconut products. We put it on a high quality standard for our consumers from all over the globe. We will make sure you will have the best experience for our coconut products.
